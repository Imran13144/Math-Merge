Game Rules

In English:

1. Swipe the screen to move and merge similar numbers together.

2. Merging two identical numbers creates a bigger number and rewards points.

3. In Pro mode, keep track of time. Time is limited for each level!

4. Match tiles diagonally, horizontally, or vertically by drawing lines.

हिंदी में:

1. समान नंबरों को एक साथ le jane aur jodne ke liye screen ko swipe karein.

2. Do saman numberon ko milane se ek bada number banta hai aur points milte hain.

3. Pro mode mein samay ka dhyan rakhein. Pratyek level ke liye samay seemit hai!

4. Line kheenchkar tiles ko tircha, aada ya seedha milaya ja sakta hai.
